I created this logo using Canva. The dark circle background represents a vast and unexplored world, waiting to be discovered. The image of a boy flying in white symbolizes freedom and boundless potential. 'Yes to new adventures' in pink signifies the joy of discovery. The designed font adds a playful and adventurous touch to the text, reflecting the excitement that adventure tours aim to provide.

I selected the movie "Nerve" as a hypothetical design project because it offers a visually compelling and thematically rich source of inspiration for a book cover or magazine cover. "Nerve" is known for its thrilling and high-stakes storyline, where an online dare game takes center stage. The combination of the dark and mysterious dares set in an urban cityscape, the iconic dare wheel, and the characters Vee and Ian all provide excellent elements for a striking and attention-grabbing design. Furthermore, the themes of technology, risk, and the blurred lines between reality and the virtual world make it an intriguing choice that can resonate with readers. The cover design can capture the essence of these elements while evoking the tension and excitement of the movie.
