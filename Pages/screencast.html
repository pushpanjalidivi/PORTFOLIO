This is my EME 6208 design and development e-portfolio. Enjoy!

The screencast focuses on demonstrating the process of recalling an email in Outlook, specifically when accessed through a web browser. This is a highly useful skill for anyone who uses Outlook for professional or personal communication, offering a solution for those moments when an email is sent prematurely or to the wrong recipient. While recording the screencast in Camtasia, I added a step-by-step voiceover narration. This was done to ensure clarity and ease of understanding, guiding viewers through each phase of the email recall process. The narration was carefully scripted to be concise yet informative, maintaining a steady pace that aligns with the on-screen actions.

To enhance the educational value of the screencast, I employed Annotations effects such as cursor highlighting in Camtasia. This technique draws the viewer's attention to crucial actions and buttons, making it easier to follow along and understand each step. I used strategic blurring effects to obscure any sensitive information displayed in my Outlook during the tutorial, ensuring privacy and focus on the tutorial content. Additionally, tinting effects were applied to improve the visual appeal and clarity of the screencast, making the instructional content more engaging. To add an extra layer of appeal, I carefully integrated background music into the screencast. After completing the screencast, I exported the final product and uploaded it to YouTube.
